Seattle Food Access Map
Citations

Primary dataset
- City of Seattle Human Services Department. Emergency Food and Meals Seattle and King County. Seattle Open Data, dataset ID kkzf-ntnu.
- Local project copy: assets/rows.geojson
- Publisher listed in metadata: data.seattle.gov
- Attribution link in dataset metadata: http://www.seattle.gov/humanservices/

Wealth layer datasets
- Local project copy: data/wealth_neighborhoods.geojson
- Local project copy: data/wealth_boundaries.geojson
- City of Seattle GIS. Community Reporting Areas. Seattle Open Data / ArcGIS Hub.
- Download source for the local boundary copy: https://services.arcgis.com/ZOyb2t4B0UYuYNYH/arcgis/rest/services/Community_Reporting_Areas_ACS5Y10/FeatureServer/0/query
- Seattle comparison benchmark in the hover panel is computed from the local CRA income dataset above as a weighted citywide average household income.

Basemap
- OpenStreetMap contributors. Tile attribution shown in seattle_food_map.html.
